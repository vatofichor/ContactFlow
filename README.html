Core Features

ContactFlow is built so you never have to wait for a page to load. Everything you need to document, research, and escalate a call is available instantly right from your workspace.

Case Notes Editor

A built-in editor specifically designed for taking live call notes. It features a formatting toolbar for lists and bold text, and a handy Find & Replace tool to quickly fix typos before you copy your final notes.

ScratchNotes

Your personal template drawer. Save your most-used greetings, troubleshooting steps, or closing statements here. Open it instantly using the shortcut, click the note you need, and it drops right into your case.

Command Launcher

Press Alt + / anywhere to bring up the command palette. It's the fastest way to trigger a specific tool or jump to a section of the page without touching your mouse.

ScratchBoard

A safe, temporary workspace. When you need to sanitize customer data, strip weird formatting from an email, or just stage some text, use the command scratchboard to pull this editor up.

Integrated Tools (Your daily drivers)

ContactFlow has several built-in modules designed specifically to speed up your most common tasks.

🔍 AI Assist

A dedicated research panel to help you look up complex policies, hardware specs, or knowledge articles without having to open new tabs or lose your place in your current case notes.

📚 Help & Guides

The Help-Panel acts as your quick-reference hub. It includes searchable documentation and direct links out to the support portals you need most.

⚡ Speed & Safety

Because ContactFlow is so incredibly fast, it saves your templates locally to your browser. Always remember to use the Export buttons in ScratchNotes and ScratchBoard to back up your custom work in case your browser cache gets cleared!

Keyboard Shortcuts

Master these to fly through your workflow without lifting your hands from the keyboard.

Shortcut Action
Alt + / Open the Global Command Palette
Alt + i Open your ScratchNotes templates
Alt + n Jump down to the Case Notes editor
Alt + c Copy your entire Case Note to your clipboard
Alt + t Open the Phonetic Alphabet helper
Alt + y Open the CX Survey script
Alt + u Open the Escalation form
Alt + h Toggle the Find & Replace tool
